
Standby mode .cmd



To activate standby mode, copy and paste this file as a shortcut.
Then right click properties,
set your own shortcut key and icon
After your explorer.exe or system interface process is killed,
press ctrl+shift+esc > file > Run
type explorer.exe and hit enter to continue
Click screen off to turn off your display at least 1 second after you click standby mode .cmd

Taskkill /im explorer.exe /f
